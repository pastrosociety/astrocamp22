INTRODUCTION

This three days residential Astronomy Camp is designed by the Pokhara Astronomical Society (PAS) to enhance the high school student’s understanding of astronomy and space science with a practical approach. The objective of the workshop is to bring skill sets and practical knowledge to students and teachers during classroom teaching activities. This workshop is an international standard and will run smoothly as per the schedule.
