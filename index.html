INTRODUCTION

Three Days Hands-on activities are designed by the Pokhara Astronomical Society (PAS) to enhance the high school student’s understanding of astronomy and space science with a practical approach. The objective of the workshop is to bring skill sets and practical knowledge to students and teachers during classroom teaching activities. This workshop is an international standard and will run smoothly as per the schedule.
This is a preliminary form for the interested teachers and schools who wish to attend this Astronomy Camp. Schools will be invited to participate later after selection. Two students and one science teacher will participate from a school.
